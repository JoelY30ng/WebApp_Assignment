Percussion Family

Contrary to belief, the percussion family does'nt consist of only drums, they also consist of many other instruments, from small ones, like triangles and cymbals, to bigger ones like marimbas and xylophones. Percussion instruments are used to keep rhythm, make special sounds (e.g. sound of thunder), and add excitement and colour to the music performed.Percussion instruments consist of any instrument that makes a sound when hit, shaken or scraped.
