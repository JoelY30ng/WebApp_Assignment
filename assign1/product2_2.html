  1. Description

    Originating in the 19th century, ukeleles are the Hawaiian versions of the Portuguese machete (small guitars) introduced by Portuguese immigrants going into the region. It usually consists of four nylon or gut strings or four courses of strings. Some strings may be paired in courses, giving the instrument a total of six or eight strings. The Kala KA-15S ukelele is well known for its amazing quality and sound, all in an affordable price range. This model is perfect for beginner players who want to try their hand on ukeleles, or for experienced players who want just want a reliable ukelele to perform with.
